Pricing

At Move It, we make furniture moving simple, stress-free, and budget-friendly. Whether you're relocating a single piece, moving into a new apartment, or shifting an entire office, our pricing is designed to be clear and competitive — with no hidden fees. We charge based on the distance, size of the move, and any additional services you select. With flexible options for every kind of move, you’ll only pay for what you need.
